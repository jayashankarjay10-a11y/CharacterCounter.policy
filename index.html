Thank you for using the Character Counter browser extension! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the extension.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Character Counter extension is designed to be privacy-first. It operates entirely locally within your browser.

  • Text Selections: When you highlight text on a webpage and use the extension to calculate statistics, the text is processed locally on your device. Your selected text is never sent to external servers, stored, logged, or shared with anyone.
  • User Preferences: The extension saves your preferred "Display Mode" (e.g., Characters, Words, Numbers) so you don't have to select it every time. This preference is saved using your browser's built-in storage syncing capabilities (like your synced Google account in Chrome). We as developers do not have access to this data.

2. Permissions Used

To function correctly, the extension requires certain browser permissions. Here is how they are used:

  • Storage: Used exclusively to save your "Display Mode" preference so the extension remembers your choice between uses. This data is kept locally on your browser or synced with your browser profile.
  • Notifications: The extension uses your browser's native notification system solely to alert you when you try to use the counter on a restricted page (like the Chrome Web Store or internal Chrome settings pages). These notifications are triggered locally on your device.
  • Context Menus & Scripting: Used strictly to create the right-click menu and display the results box over the text you highlight on standard webpages.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not collect, store, sell, rent, or share any personal information, browsing history, or usage data. Because the extension functions entirely on your device, we simply do not possess any of your data to share.

4. Third-Party Services

The Character Counter extension does not include any third-party analytics tools, external tracking scripts, or external APIs.

5. Your Rights

Since all data (your display preference) is stored locally or tied to your personal browser sync, you have full control over it. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser's extension data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if new features are added to the extension that change how data is handled. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
